About

Kevyn is a Full-Stack Software Engineer with 15+ years of experience architecting and implementing enterprise applications. Expertise in UI/UX design and development, modernizing legacy systems, mentoring, and implementing best DevOps practices.

He is very passionate about emerging technologies, with a growing focus on AI/ML, including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), large language models (LLMs), and speech synthesis. Kevyn is always eager to learn, adapt, and explore how advancements in AI and software development are shaping the future.

He is also a maker, with an endless stream of ideas for new and innovative creations combining custom software and prototype hardware. He is especially interested in the ESP32 line of microcontrollers from Espressif and their usefulness in home automation, media gadgets, and remote sensing/monitoring.
